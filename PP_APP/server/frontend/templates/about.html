MISSION

 

Large online retailers ship millions of packages each day, and they often use packaging that is both wasteful and harmful. They do so to reduce costs and as a result their packaging is excessive and contains non-biodegradable materials, such as plastic. Ultimately, the environment pays the price as shipping materials go to waste. The aim of our project is to draw attention to these practices.

We created a system that uses images to automatically detect oversized shipping boxes and harmful materials. Our method uses computer vision techniques to analyze the images and calculate a packaging grade. With our results we present the companies who used the most efficient packaging, informing consumers of retailer practices and their impact on the environment. We hope this project promotes change for more efficient packaging practices.

We were happy to have the opportunity to work on a project that we found interesting, which also supported an environmental cause.
