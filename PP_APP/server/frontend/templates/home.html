Some facts...

 

  • 80% of all products sold in the USA and EU are packaged in cardboard

  • In the USA alone 850 million tons of cardboard are thrown away annually

  • That is equivalent to 1 billion trees

  • Recycling cardboard can be toxic and inefficient

  • 40% of plastic used is for packaging and it is only used once!
